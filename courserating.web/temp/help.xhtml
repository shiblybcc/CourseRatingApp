About

This website is intended to help new students as well as existing students to select their courses. In RWTH University students from different countries come to study. Most cases the study system is not similar with their home country. Keeping that in mind we developed this website to help those students.

Students who take a course in a semester can rate a lecture in different category like whether the professor was good at his lecture or not, whether the exercises was easy or not or if it was helpful or not etc. Students can also write their review as comment for the course. And also other users can up vote or down vote the comments which explains whether the comment was useful or not.

So if any student is confused with so many courses and which one to choose from them, can visit our website and have a look at the courses that are easy, useful and popular to other students.

How to Authenticate

To rate or comment a lecture first you have to authenticate using your RWTH campus office credentials. First you have to click authenticate button.



After that you will redirected to to another page where you have to enter your credentials.



If you enter correct credentials then you will be redirected to this page



After you click authorize you can start rating and commenting the courses you are taking this semester.

How to Rate

To rate a lecture first you have to authenticate as shown above. Then click "Rate" at the right of each lecture name.



A popup will appear as below



You can rate as excellent, very good, good, fair etc and you have five categories like lecture, exercise, lecturer, lecture materials and additional resources. Then click ok and your rating will be saved in our system. Please be honest and don't spam the system.

How to Comment

To comment or review a lecture first you have to authenticate as shown above. Then select a lecture you want to comment. For example, if you want to comment or review "Applied Automata Theory" click on that lecture.



Then click on "New Comment" button.



After that A popup will appear as below



You have to give a title of your comment and write your comment. Click ok to save your comment. Please be honest and don't spam the system.

You can also like or dislike a comment. If someone gave spam review about a lecture or if you think the review he wrote is not correct then you can down vote it. Or if you think the review is helpful for you and might be helpful for others then you can also up vote as well.
